Consolidated cargoes (LCL)

In 2018 ESTIW arranged more than 2400 consignments from all over the world LCL Volume increases twice annually
  • Our Agent will take your cargo to CFS (Container Freight Station) specialized warehouses
  • Few customers cargoes collected into one container
  • The Consignee of the whole container is Estiw, saving you the cost of freight and cargo storage
  • We will arrange the delivery of combined cargo to your specified address (DOOR-TO-DOOR)
LCL enables importers to ship smaller amounts of cargo that’s not of a large enough volume to make FCL a viable option. This means that few shipper’s cargo is combined with other shipping consignments headed for the same destination.
Unlike FCL, which usually has a flat rate per container, LCL is charged based on the volume, set in cubic meters. LCL shipping is a cost-effective solution for smaller shipments as you only need to pay for the volume space used. At the same time it is almost always cheaper than air freight.
